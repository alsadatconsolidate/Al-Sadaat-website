About Us

At Al-Sadat Consolidate, we redefine architectural and engineering excellence with cutting-edge innovation and precision. Founded by Israr Ali Shah, an expert in architecture, civil engineering, BIM modeling, and structural design, our firm brings over 12 years of experience in delivering high-quality design, documentation, and execution solutions across multiple sectors.

We specialize in architectural planning, master planning, multi-family dwelling design, permit documentation, Revit and CAD drafting, BIM modeling, and interior design. Our expertise extends to Australian building regulations, green building certifications (LEED & Passive House), and high-demand future skills in construction technology.

Our portfolio includes landmark projects such as the NSTP Tower (Islamabad), Babri Masjid (Islamabad), Jeddah Opera House, and master planning of a 127 Kanal land development. Additionally, we provide technical drafting for exhibition booths, precise quantity take-offs, and bunker design solutions.

With a commitment to excellence, innovation, and sustainability, Al-Sadat Consolidate transforms concepts into reality, ensuring precision-driven execution that meets global standards.
